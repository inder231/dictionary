Keyboard

/'ki:b…î:d/

noun

Meaning

  • (etc.) A set of keys used to operate a typewriter, computer etc.
  • A component of many instruments including the piano, oran, and harpsichord consisting of usually black and white keys that cause different tones to be produced when struck.
  • A device with keys of a musical keyboard, used to control electronic sound-producing devieces which may be build into or separate from the keyboard device.

Synonyms electornic keyboard
